System Settings & Documentation

The Dog Monitoring System (DMS) is an integrated health and behavioral assessment tool. It combines visual data analysis with physical health metrics to provide a comprehensive "Stress & Health Score" for dogs.

Step 1: Registration

Captures basic profile data (Name, Breed, Age). This establishes the baseline for the specific dog being monitored.

Step 2: Video Upload

The core AI/Monitoring component. Users upload video footage which is processed to observe movement patterns and behavioral cues.

Step 3: Vitals Check

A questionnaire-based evaluation of physical vital signs (skin health, fur condition, presence of parasites, overall body condition (weight) and body language ) to supplement the video data.

  • 1.0 - 1.5 Excellent Health
  • 1.6 - 2.5 Fair / Mild Stress
  • 2.6 - 3.5 Poor / High Stress
  • 3.6 - 4.0 Critical Attention
